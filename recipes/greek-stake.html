Greek Steak Marinade

Description

This Greek steak marinade, with just a few ingredients, delivers flavorful steaks worthy enough to serve to guests. It's quick to prepare and perfect for enhancing your favorite cuts of beef.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh oregano
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose Greek seasoning (such as Cavender'sÂ®)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk extra virgin olive oil, oregano, Greek seasoning, salt, and pepper in a small bowl until smooth.
  2. Pour marinade over steaks, ensuring all sides are coated. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours.
